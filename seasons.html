Spring Planting

  • Start seeds indoors 6-8 weeks before last frost
  • Prepare garden beds when soil is workable
  • Plant cool-season crops like lettuce and peas
  • Transplant seedlings after last frost date
  • Apply compost and organic fertilizer

Summer Care

  • Water deeply in early morning
  • Harvest frequently to encourage production
  • Watch for pests and diseases
  • Provide shade for heat-sensitive plants
  • Start fall crops indoors

Fall Gardening

  • Plant garlic and overwintering crops
  • Harvest remaining summer crops
  • Plant cover crops to protect soil
  • Clean up garden debris
  • Prepare beds for next spring

Seasonal Gardening Tips

  • Spring: Focus on soil preparation and early planting
  • Summer: Maintain consistent watering and pest control
  • Fall: Plant garlic and prepare for winter
  • Winter: Plan next year's garden and order seeds
  • Year-Round: Keep a garden journal for reference
