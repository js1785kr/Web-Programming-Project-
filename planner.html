Garden Planner

Essential planning tips for your garden

Garden Planning Basics

  • Choose a location with 6-8 hours of sunlight daily
  • Test soil pH before planting (ideal: 6.0-7.0 for most vegetables)
  • Prepare soil by adding 2-3 inches of compost
  • Plan layout with tall plants on north side to avoid shading
  • Leave walking paths between garden beds

Plant Spacing Guidelines

  • Tomatoes: Space 24-36 inches apart
  • Carrots: Space 2-3 inches apart
  • Lettuce: Space 8-12 inches apart
  • Beans: Space 4-6 inches apart
  • Cucumbers: Space 12-24 inches apart
  • Radishes: Space 1-2 inches apart

Companion Planting Guide

  • Tomatoes grow well with basil, carrots, and onions
  • Carrots grow well with tomatoes, lettuce, and rosemary
  • Cucumbers grow well with beans, corn, and radishes
  • Marigolds repel pests when planted throughout garden
  • Avoid planting tomatoes with cabbage or potatoes

Seasonal Planting Schedule

  • Early Spring: Lettuce, spinach, peas, radishes
  • Late Spring: Tomatoes, peppers, beans, corn
  • Summer: Cucumbers, squash, melons, okra
  • Early Fall: Kale, broccoli, carrots, beets
  • Late Fall: Garlic, onions, cover crops

📝 My Garden Notes

📐 Layout Planning Tips

  • Use the "square foot gardening" method for small spaces
  • Rotate crops each year to prevent soil diseases
  • Group plants with similar water needs together
  • Include pollinator-friendly flowers for better yields
  • Plan for succession planting to extend harvest season

💡 Gardening Tips

  • Plant complementary plants together
  • Rotate crops each season
  • Water plants early in the morning
  • Use organic compost
