Pest Control

Identify and treat common garden pests effectively.

Aphids

Small insects that feed on plant sap. They cluster on new growth and undersides of leaves.

Neem oil or insecticidal soap

Slugs & Snails

Feed on leaves at night, leaving irregular holes and slime trails.

Beer traps or copper tape

Caterpillars

Larvae that eat plant leaves, can defoliate plants quickly.

Hand pick or use BT spray

Spider Mites

Tiny arachnids that cause stippling and webbing on leaves.

Strong water spray or neem oil

Prevention Tips

  • Keep garden clean of debris
  • Use row covers
  • Introduce beneficial insects
  • Rotate crops
  • Water in the morning

Organic Solutions

  • Neem oil
  • Insecticidal soap
  • Diatomaceous earth
  • Companion planting
  • Homemade sprays
